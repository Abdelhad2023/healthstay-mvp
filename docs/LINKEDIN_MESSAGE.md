# HST-OUTREACH-01 — LinkedIn Messages

---

## 1. Connection Request Message (300 character limit)

Hello [Name], I am working on an independent digital concept to improve the international patient journey for Swiss neuro-health stays. I have a working prototype and would value the perspective of someone with your experience. Would you be open to connecting?

---

## 2. Follow-up After Connection Acceptance

Thank you for connecting. To briefly introduce what I am working on:

I have developed a controlled prototype focused on the international patient journey for premium Swiss neuro-health — prevention, second opinion, and rehabilitation. The concept is structured around human review, clinic validation, and transparent packages, with AI used only for programme orientation.

I am currently seeking feedback from Swiss neuro-health professionals and would welcome 20 minutes to show you the prototype and hear your thoughts. Would that be of interest?

---

Would you be open to a 20-minute call to see the prototype and discuss whether a pilot might be relevant for your organisation?

---

## Usage Notes

- Keep messages under 150 words — LinkedIn is read quickly
- Do not include links in initial messages (LinkedIn may flag them)
- Wait for acceptance before sending the longer message
- Do not mention Cereneo or any specific provider
- Use "independent concept" and "working prototype" — not "startup" or "company"
- If the recipient asks "are you partnered with X?", answer clearly: "No, this is an independent concept. I am seeking feedback and pilot discussion."
