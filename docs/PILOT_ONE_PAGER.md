# HST-OUTREACH-01 — Pilot Concept One-Pager

> For discussion purposes only. Independent concept — not affiliated with any specific provider.

---

## Title

**HealthStay — A structured digital journey concept for premium Swiss neuro-health international patients.**

---

## One-Sentence Concept

An independent, controlled digital prototype that demonstrates how international patients could explore Swiss neuro-health programmes, request transparent proposals, and receive human-guided support from first inquiry to post-stay follow-up — with clinic validation at every clinical decision point.

---

## Problem

- International patients considering a Swiss neuro-health stay face fragmented information, unclear package structures, and limited coordination before and after their stay
- Hospital websites are informative but not designed as journey-oriented platforms
- Families helping a relative abroad need clear steps, transparent expectations, and human support — not just a brochure
- Providers spend significant time answering repetitive administrative questions and qualifying enquiries

---

## Proposed Pilot

A six-month collaborative exploration to evaluate whether a structured digital journey platform adds value for a Swiss neuro-health provider and its international patients. The pilot begins with prototype review and feedback, progresses through journey refinement, and concludes with a controlled evaluation phase.

---

## What the Prototype Demonstrates

- Transparent programme pages with package structure and inclusions
- Care Matching Preview — assisted orientation based on patient preferences (AI, not diagnosis)
- Human-reviewed request flow — every enquiry reviewed by a coordinator before reaching the clinic
- Structured 10-step patient journey from discovery to post-stay follow-up
- Side-by-side programme comparison
- Trust and safety framework with nine principles
- Provider value proposition page
- Six-month pilot roadmap

---

## What It Does Not Do

- No medical diagnosis, advice, treatment recommendation, or outcome prediction
- No AI involvement in clinical decision-making
- No instant booking — all requests require human coordinator review and clinic validation
- No storage of patient medical data
- No claims of partnership with any specific provider
- No replacement for medical teams — the platform is a journey layer around the clinical path
- No commercial marketplace or booking engine

---

## Value for a Provider

- Better-qualified international patient enquiries arriving through a structured request format
- Reduced administrative back-and-forth — patients self-educate before submitting a request
- Premium digital presence aligned with Swiss quality standards
- Coordinator pre-screening before enquiries reach clinical staff
- Structured pre-arrival logistics coordination
- Post-stay follow-up framework for improved patient continuity
- Reusable programme structure adaptable for future health offerings

---

## Six-Month Pilot Idea

| Phase | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| Month 1–2 | Prototype review, feedback, content refinement |
| Month 3–4 | Journey and request workflow finalisation |
| Month 5 | Controlled pilot with selected patient enquiries |
| Month 6 | Evaluation, improvement identification, scale decision |

The provider remains in full control at all times. The pilot can be paused or stopped at any point.
