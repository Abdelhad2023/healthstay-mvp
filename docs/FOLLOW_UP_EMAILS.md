## Usage Notes

- Wait 5–7 days between follow-ups
- Maximum 2 follow-ups if no response, then stop
- After a meeting, send the thank-you email within 24 hours
- Do not pressure for a decision — the goal is relationship and feedback
- Always offer a clear way out: "If this is not the right time, I understand"
