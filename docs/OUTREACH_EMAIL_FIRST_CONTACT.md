## Version A — Main Email (Recommended)

**To:** [Provider contact name]
**Subject:** Independent digital pilot concept for Swiss neuro-health international patient journeys

Dear [Name],

I am reaching out as someone preparing a strategic digital health concept focused on the international patient journey for premium Swiss neuro-health, prevention, second opinion, and rehabilitation stays.

The idea is straightforward: many international patients and their families struggle with fragmented information, unclear pricing, and lack of coordination when considering a health stay abroad. Existing hospital websites are informative but not designed as journey-oriented platforms.

I have built a controlled working prototype that demonstrates how a structured, transparent, human-reviewed digital journey could work — from initial discovery and programme comparison through to clinic validation and post-stay follow-up.

Key principles of the concept:
- Every request is reviewed by a human coordinator before reaching a clinic
- AI is used only for programme orientation, never for diagnosis or treatment decisions
- No patient medical data is stored in the prototype
- The platform does not replace medical teams — it supports the patient journey around the clinical path

I would welcome the opportunity to present this concept in a 20–30 minute online meeting, gather your feedback, and explore whether a small six-month pilot discussion would be relevant for your organisation.

Would you be open to a brief conversation in the coming weeks?

Warm regards,

- No patient data stored

I am now at a stage where candid feedback from an experienced Swiss neuro-health provider would be invaluable. I would be grateful for 25 minutes to walk you through the prototype and explore whether a pilot discussion could be relevant.
